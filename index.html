Rule Book

  • The goal is simple, work your way through each of the difficulties below:

    Group Stage - Easy , 12 cards, 6 matches needed

    Knockout Stage - Medium, 18 cards, 9 matches needed

    Cup Final - Hard, 24 cards, 12 matches needed

  • Click on a card to make it flip around. Click on a second card to try find its match!
    If the cards match, they will stay face up, if not, they will flip around and you can try again!

  • Test yourself against the clock and try to find all the matches as quickly as you can!
